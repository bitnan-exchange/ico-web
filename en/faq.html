BRT is a token issued by the ICO.In addition to trading in the parallel market,the holder will regularly receive bitnan profit sharing.

When does the BRT issue start?

BRT issue will start from 20:00 September 1, 2017, to the end of the 19:59 September 30th,

When can I receive the BRT?

BRT's release is based on the Ethereum smart contract, which sends the corresponding amount of BRT to your source address in real time when your transfer accounts is received.

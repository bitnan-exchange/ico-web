Abstract of bitnan

Aim to create the world's best digital currency trading platform

Bitnan is a profit sharing digital currency trading platform.
Provides a variety of investment tools including spot transaction, margin trading and financing trading,
as well as graphical programming tools and quantitative trading module.
Bitnan's profits will be shared with every BRT holder.

The advantage of bitnan

For bitnan users

  1. Spot transaction is free of charge to maximize thie investment return.
  2. Provide a variety of investment tools to meet different investment needs.
  3. Provide graphical programming tools and quantitative transaction module.
  4. Efficient and stable RESTful API.
  5. Supply investment advice and value-added services to users by investment assistance APP.

For ICO participants

  1. BRT holders will share platform profits according to their BRT holding amount.
  2. BRT can trade on bitnan and other digital currency trading platforms to achieve a premium.
  3. The platform will aperiodicity buy back BRT at the issue price as an internal reserve.
  4. The platform will cooperate with financial institutions from online to offline, establish brand image, provide various value-added services.

Profit sharing

After bitnan formally release, at the end of each quarter, we will calculate the profit of bitnan.
With guarantee the normal operation of bitnan, we will share the residual profit with BRT holder at the ratio of 1:1.
The profit allocation will be done automatically by bitnan.
